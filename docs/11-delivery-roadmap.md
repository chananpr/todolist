# 11 Delivery Roadmap

## MVP
- auth
- employee CRUD with generated username and password
- department and team management
- RBAC foundation
- project CRUD
- task and subtask CRUD
- board movement
- S3 upload foundation
- activity logging
- dashboard basics
- AI plan draft generation with manual approval

## Phase 2
- time tracking
- KPI snapshot jobs
- cost dashboards
- review and approval workflow
- AI assignee recommendations
- AI daily and weekly planning
- notifications
- richer calendar and scheduling

## Advanced
- configurable workflow engine
- multi-step approvals
- SSO and enterprise identity integration
- data warehouse export
- AI copilot with contextual task actions
- cross-project capacity planning
